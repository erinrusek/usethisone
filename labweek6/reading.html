Starting JavaScript



Although pretty long, Paul Ford’s “What is Code?” was a really well-rounded analysis of the modern coder. I really
appreciated his explanation of the symbiosis between coders and resources like stackoverflow and W3. Although
not mentioned specifically, it made me feel better about looking for help and adding onto other peoples’ code
in order to learn. However, I did feel a little overwhelmed when Ford went into the ever-changing nature of
programming languages. I’m already struggling with JavaScript, so I can’t imagine coding full-time, trying to
teach myself another completely new language while still trying to grasp onto the others.

Regarding JavaScript, I feel pretty stressed out with it right now. It might be because I struggle with looking at the
whole picture, but I just get so caught up with the concept of the function. My main issue lately is trying to
call the correct Id or class, and having other functions override them. That was a big problem with this week’s
project, but however nervous I am to use JS, I am excited to learn more in order to become confident with it.
